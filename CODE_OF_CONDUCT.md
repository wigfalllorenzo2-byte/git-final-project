# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone. We are committed to creating a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Showing empathy toward others

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Disruptive or inappropriate conduct

## Our Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing the standards outlined in this document. They may take appropriate action, including warnings or removal of contributions.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when representing the project.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated fairly.
